✅ DO These
✅Attend all antenatal visits at the hospital
✅Take iron and folic acid tablets daily
✅Sleep on your left side to improve blood flow
✅Drink 8–10 cups of clean water daily
✅Use insecticide-treated bed nets (malaria prevention)
✅Wash hands before eating and after toilet
✅Do light exercise like walking 30 mins daily
✅Get tetanus injection as scheduled
❌ DON'T Do These
❌Don't take any medication without doctor's advice
❌Don't carry heavy loads or do strenuous work
❌Don't smoke or inhale smoke near others
❌Don't skip meals — baby needs nutrition 3x daily
❌Don't ignore warning signs like heavy bleeding
❌Don't deliver at home — go to hospital for delivery
❌Don't drink alcohol or traditional brews

⚠️ Go to hospital IMMEDIATELY if you have:

🔴 Heavy vaginal bleeding 🔴 Severe headache or blurred vision 🔴 Face, hands or feet severely swollen 🔴 Baby not moving for 12+ hours 🔴 High fever above 38°C 🔴 Severe stomach pains or cramps 🔴 Fits/convulsions (eclampsia)
