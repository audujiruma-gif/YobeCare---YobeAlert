## ❗ The Problem
Yobe State faces two critical gaps that technology can solve:

**1. Healthcare Access**
- Lowest doctor-to-patient ratio in Nigeria
- Most residents travel hours to reach a specialist
- Language barriers — many do not speak English
- Highest maternal mortality rates in the country

**2. Security Reporting**
- No safe, anonymous channel to report threats
- People fear identification and retaliation
- No system routing reports to the right agency
- Infrastructure damage goes unreported for weeks

---

## ✅ The Solution

### 🏥 YobeCare — AI Telemedicine App (Prototype)
A smartphone web app connecting Yobe residents to healthcare in their own language.

**Features:**
- 🎙️ Voice AI Assistant — ask health questions in Hausa, Kanuri, Fulfulde or English
- 📹 Doctor Booking — real WhatsApp booking request to medical coordinator
- 🗺️ Facilities Finder — tap-to-call + Google Maps directions for all 17 LGAs
- 🤰 Maternal Care Tracker — week-by-week pregnancy guidance
- 🚨 Disease Alerts — 20 diseases with AI-powered Q&A (Claude AI)
- 🌍 4 Languages — Hausa · Kanuri · Fulfulde · English
- 📱 Works on any smartphone, 2G/3G compatible

---

### 🛡️ YobeAlert — Anonymous Security Reporting (LIVE)
A fully deployed website for anonymous emergency and security reporting.

**Features:**
- 📝 Anonymous report form — no login, no identity stored
- 💬 Instant WhatsApp alert to emergency coordinator
- 📧 Email notification via Formspree
- 6 incident categories — Security, Abduction, Road, Medical, Infrastructure, Other
- 📞 All Yobe State emergency hotlines (199, 193, 112, 122)
- 🌍 Covers all 17 LGAs of Yobe State
- ✅ LIVE and working right now
